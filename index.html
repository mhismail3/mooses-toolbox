About

What is this?

A collection of small, focused browser-based tools. Each tool runs entirely in your browser — no files are uploaded to any server.

Privacy

All tools process data locally on your device. Your files and data never leave your browser.

Adding Tools

Each tool lives in its own folder under tools/. Check the _template/ folder for a starter template to create new tools.
