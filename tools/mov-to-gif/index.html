About MOV to GIF

What does this tool do?

Convert MOV and MP4 video files to animated GIFs entirely in your browser. Perfect for creating shareable animations from screen recordings, video clips, or any short video content.

How to Use

  1. Drop a video file onto the upload area, or click to browse
  2. Adjust the output settings:
    • Width ‚Äî Controls the GIF dimensions (height scales proportionally)
    • Framerate ‚Äî How many frames per second (lower = smaller file)
    • Quality ‚Äî Color accuracy (higher = better colors, larger file)
  3. Click "Convert to GIF" and wait for processing
  4. Download your GIF or adjust settings and reconvert

Tips for Smaller Files

  • Reduce the output width (480px or less works great)
  • Use a lower framerate (5-10 FPS is usually sufficient)
  • Keep videos short ‚Äî GIFs grow quickly with duration

Privacy

Your videos are processed entirely in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server. This tool is 100% client-side.

Supported Formats

MOV, MP4, and M4V video files are supported. The exact codec support depends on your browser.
