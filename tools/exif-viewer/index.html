About EXIF Viewer

What is EXIF?

EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) is metadata embedded in photos by digital cameras and smartphones. It includes information like:

  • Camera ‚Äî Make, model, lens info
  • Settings ‚Äî Aperture, shutter speed, ISO, focal length
  • Date & Time ‚Äî When the photo was taken
  • GPS ‚Äî Location coordinates (if enabled)
  • Image ‚Äî Dimensions, orientation, color space

How to Use

  1. Drop a JPEG or TIFF image onto the upload area, or click to browse
  2. View the extracted EXIF data organized by category
  3. Click the GPS coordinates to open in Google Maps
  4. Use "Copy All Data" to copy the metadata as text

Privacy

Your images are processed entirely in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server. This tool is 100% client-side.

Supported Formats

JPEG and TIFF images are supported. PNG, GIF, and WebP files typically don't contain EXIF data.
